HOOK SCORE — Judge only the first 3 seconds of the clip:

  • Strong emotional reaction — shock, anger, laughter, disbelief
  • Claim that challenges conventional wisdom
  • A number or stat that shocks ("I lost $50,000 in one day")

EMOTION SCORE — Judge the energy arc and sentiment peak of the full clip:
  • Visible spike in pace, emphasis, or repetition
  • **SENTIMENT PEAK: Look for the most intense emotional shift or "breakthrough" moment.**
  • Genuine vulnerability, frustration, excitement, or conviction
  • Unexpected or self-deprecating humor
  • A passionate rant or strong opinion

CLARITY SCORE — Judge standalone comprehension:
  • A viewer who has NEVER seen the full video must understand this clip
  • It must feel like a complete, self-contained thought
  • Penalize clips that open mid-sentence, reference something unexplained,
    or end abruptly without resolution

STORY SCORE — Judge narrative completeness:
  • Setup → Tension → Payoff must exist within the clip itself
  • The clip must end on a strong note, not mid-thought
  • Penalize clips that trail off or require the full video for context

VIRALITY SCORE — Judge shareability:
  • Would someone forward this to a friend?
  • Does it trigger a strong agree/disagree reaction?
  • Does it teach something surprising, validate a belief, or make someone laugh?
